ACTION LIBRARY

Real interventions cross axes. Click one to apply its effect — sliders move together because biology doesn't respect column boundaries.

How to read deltas: a Zone 2 program lifts reserve directly (VO₂max, mito), lowers residualized risk indirectly (BP, lipids, A1c), and lifts lifestyle via sleep and mood. Three axes, one habit. The chart geometry and render both move.

Stress-test cases: high reserve + low lifestyle = curve bends up, line stays muted (the burned-out 50yo). Low reserve + high lifestyle = curve collapses, line stays warm (the 25yo who feels invincible). The model can show both honestly because the axes don't double-count.

Render mix is locked: brightness/saturation = 0.5·reserve + 0.5·lifestyle, floor 0.15. Risk does not affect render — only curve geometry. Reserve affects both, by design, because reserve genuinely contributes to felt vitality.
